About us

We are here to make you a better version of yourself. You can find us on many different locations in many different European countries.
Our chain of gyms opened in early 2000s, since than we had more than 10 000 clients who were more than pleased with our equitment and our personal couches.

JOINING FOR THE FIRST TIME?
No worries, you dont have to be shy about coming alone we will find you someone to show you how to use the equitment and tell you which ones you should use.
Each client, when JOINING us for the first time, which bought a monthly pass for the gym gets 7 free practices with one of the couches.
In case you have a pass for a gym in your place, dont worry, you can use it in any other of our facilities.

We are open 24/7, so if you dont have time to come during the day the whole night is ahead of you. Dont make excuses, come, practice, make new friends, invite yours to join you.
If you are overwhelmed with everything happening around you, dont keep it all in yourself, working out is the best way to let all your emotions go.
